To *get* note use /get:
`/get [chat code] [note name]`

To *remove* note use /rm:
`/rm [chat code] [note name]`

Examples:
`/get 766ad863cf57a225a055535e5ad815fb to-do-list`
`/rm 766ad863cf57a225a055535e5ad815fb to-do-list`


To *create* new note use /add:
`/add [note name]
[Title]
[Multiline
Text]`

Example:
`/add test
Look!
I can write here some text.
It's great, isn't it?`


You can download archive with your notes: /get\_archive.


After adding new notes bot sends *chat code* and *note's name*.
Use it to share your note with somebody.
